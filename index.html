The Vision

What Would You Sacrifice
for the Next Generation?

Biosphere Simulator is a game about legacy. Not conquering empires — but the intimate, human-scale story of one family's journey through all of human history.

The river flows. The current pushes your family toward the Falls — a waterfall at the edge of oblivion. Every turn, you must decide: spend your resources keeping yourself alive, or invest in your children's future.

Raise children with names, personalities, and ambitions. Arrange marriages that secure your bloodline. Watch your settlement grow from a lean-to shelter into a thriving civilization. Make choices that echo across twenty generations.

Starting on the muddy banks of prehistoric Mesopotamia and ending in the industrial age, Biosphere Simulator asks one question: can your dynasty survive the river?

Core Features

A Dynasty Simulator
With Real Depth

The River of Time

A horizontal river flows relentlessly toward the Falls. Four channels represent social standing — from scraping by in the Shallows to ruling from the Royal Rapids. Fight the current or be consumed.

Raise Real Children

Every child has a name, personality, backstory, and ambitions. Teach them, send them exploring, or put them to work. Unequal treatment breeds sibling rivalry. Balanced investment builds a stable family.

Strategic Marriage

Political alliances. Love matches. Power plays. Finding the right spouse isn't a checkbox — it's a genuine dilemma that determines your dynasty's future every single generation.

Build Your Settlement

Watch your civilization evolve from a lean-to shelter to a thriving settlement. 14 building categories unlock as you craft milestones. Your choices shape the skyline.

Legacy That Compounds

Education invested in children compounds across generations. A dynasty that prioritizes knowledge early reaps massive rewards later. 42 permanent upgrades shape your family's trajectory.

70+ Narrative Events

Choose-your-own-adventure encounters with real consequences. Storms, rival clashes, suitors, ancient ruins. No optimal choice — every decision is a tradeoff that ripples across generations.

Rival Dynasties

NPC families share the river. They compete for treasures, block your path, or become powerful allies. Ambitious, cooperative, territorial, or scholarly — each rival has a personality.

Artifacts & Companions

Discover ancient artifacts with permanent dynasty bonuses. Equip items from common to legendary. Adopt pet companions — loyal hounds, temple cats, and hawks that aid your journey.

The Core Tension

Every Token Spent on Yourself
Is One Not Spent on Your Children

The Impossible Choice

The Falls are closing in. You have three tokens. Spend them to push upstream and survive — or invest in your daughter's education so she'll thrive when you're gone. This isn't a puzzle. It's a family.

Generational Compounding

A child educated in irrigation produces more tokens as an adult. Their children inherit those skills. Fifteen generations of careful investment creates a dynasty that shapes civilization. Short-term sacrifice, long-term legacy.

Permadeath With Purpose

When your elder is swept over the Falls, it means something. When a favorite child starves because you chose wrong, you feel it. Hard but fair. Every loss teaches you something for the next dynasty.
